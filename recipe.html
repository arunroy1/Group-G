Description: A delicious sample recipe with a variety of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Heat a pan and pour the batter to cook on both sides until golden brown. Serve warm.
